MASONRY

SHTS undertakes the art and craftwork of construction and fabricating in stone, clay, brick, or concrete block that brings a strong base and partition to your buildings. Masonry works are necessary when it comes to construction. Small scale or large scale, we deliver exceptional services for your masonry requirements.

Masonry is both the construction of stone or brick structures and also the process by which these structures are created. Construction and fabrication of stone, clay, brick, or concrete blocks through the art and craft of masonry. Masonry is often considered reinforced or unreinforced concrete construction.

  • Weather resistance - Masonry walls can withstand heavy storms, heat, and cold.
  • Shield from termites, rotting and fungus.
  • Maintenance-free. Masonry is a long-lasting and low maintenance material. Adding value to your construction.
  • Non-combustible property assists in fire protection
