How We Work?

After inspecting your carpet, we will employ the best carpet cleaning technique. The most common, safe, and effective method is to shampoo and sanitise the carpet.

Deep vacuuming and shampooing are effective methods for removing all types of dirt and stains. After the procedure, the carpet will be dry and chemical-free. We bring back the carpet's original freshness and condition while eliminating all odours. The involved actions are:

  • On-site Inspection

    We examine the carpet in the client's home. Our crew will inspect the rooms with carpeted floors to find any blemishes or stains. On the inspection, we will make an effort to determine the severity of the stains.

  • Vacuuming

    By vacuuming the carpet, our crew will get rid of all the sand, dirt, or other debris on the surface. To achieve the optimum results, this is essential.

  • Spot-Treatment

    We address each blemish and stain that we previously noted. To remove the stain, a cleaning product is sprayed to every area that requires special attention.

  • Shampooing

    The carpet is first shampooed by the machine to remove any dirt lodged deep inside the fibres. The shampoo is extracted, and the dirt is also removed.

  • Powerful Suction

    The machine's powerful suction will quickly dry the carpet by removing any remaining water and debris.

  • Final Inspection

    To guarantee the finest outcome and customer satisfaction, we perform a final inspection of the carpet.
